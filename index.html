Designed with you in mind

SQLViz is an easy and open source way to turn SQL into graphical visualization of data within your database. SQLViz supports Postgres and Redshift. It allows for rapid analysis and dissemination of insights locked into your data sets.

Open Source

Get started today, for free! You can rest assured that this product won't disappear tomorrow.

Scheduled Reports

Need to receive a report on a recurring basis? Setup an email schedule for it!

Fully Secure

SQLViz is a self-hosted solution so you can have full control over your data's access rights.
